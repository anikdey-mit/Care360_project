Flood

{% if session["calamity_2_severity"] == "high" %}

Risk Factor : High

Safety Information

  • Recommend your grandparents and your parents to install waterproof switches at home.
  • Ask your parents to buy rubber gloves, boots and eye protection for your grandparents
  • Along with your parents investigate whether your grandparent's house pipes are fitted with taps to control water.
  • Recommend your grandparents to have "dry flood-proofing" coatings applied to walls and doorways so floodwater does not enter the house.
  • As soon as you see crack anywhere in your grandparent's house let them know it can cause leakage of water.
  • Ask your parents to put torch, battery-operated radios and spare batteries in your grandparent's house.

Tornado

{% if session["calamity_4_severity"] == "high" %}

Risk Factor : High

Safety Information

  • Ask your parents to buy a small radio or internet-enabled device if your grandparents do not have it. It is useful in communication during a tornado.
  • Sit with your parents and grandparent to prepare an emergency plan including shelter for your grandparents.
  • Create an emergency kit for your grandparents along with your parents which should have their medication, water bottles, and non-perishable food.
  • Stick your parent's phone number within your grandparents kitchen or in an easily accessible place for them
  • Help your grandparents to remove left over items around their house frequently so they do not hurt them in a tornado.
