General Safety Recommendations



Please read the instructions below and follow these in your day to day life. Also look around your and your grandparents house and try to follow safety instructions and make you house safer.




Light Safety

Make sure there are adequate lighting in driveway,garage,walkways,at all doors,night-lights/signs giving directions to the bathroom, laundry and kitchen.

Living room safety

Make sure the floors are slip resistant and a clear, unobstructed path through the room (no clutter, cords, wires, baskets or other things to trip over)

Bedroom Safety

Make sure to remove hazardous items,such as electric blankets and hot water bottles. Make sure having wood and linoleum floors non-skid Make sure of having a light at the entrance to the room and reachable from the bed Make sure having a telephone and assistive walking devices (cane, walker, wheelchair) within reach of the bed.

Kitchen Safety

Make sure Has access to the stove been controlled (knobs removed, lock on oven door, stove connected to hidden circuit breaker or gas valve), Make sure have all drawers and cabinets with safe objects labeled, Make sure Have sharp knives and other dangerous implements been removed or locked up.

Bathroom Safety

Make sure have all medicines and non-electric razors been put away, Make sure a shower or bath seat accessible to allow a person to direct the flow of water desired

Shower Safety

make sure a non-skid bathmat in the bathtub Make sure a tub or shower seat.

Laundry Safety

Make sure having a non-slip floor surface.

Toilet Safety

Make sure sturdy grab bars at the toilet (or toilet arms and a raised seat) Make sure the toilet seat in good condition and securely fastened Make sure toilet paper easily reachable from the toilet seat.
